# Code of Conduct

Project participants must communicate respectfully, discuss technical decisions
in good faith, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or disclosure of
private information.

Maintainers may edit or remove abusive contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior creates an unsafe or
unproductive environment.

Report conduct concerns privately through the security contact method described
in `SECURITY.md`. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and without
retaliation.
